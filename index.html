Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers. Here are the most common questions I receive about my web development services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on complexity. A simple landing page takes 1-2 weeks, while a full e-commerce site with custom features can take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Are your websites mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Every website I build is fully responsive and optimized for all devices - mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. I follow mobile-first design principles to ensure great user experience across all screen sizes.

Do you handle SEO optimization?

Yes! I implement technical SEO best practices including proper meta tags, structured data, fast loading speeds, and mobile optimization. I can also help with content strategy and keyword research to improve your search rankings.

What technologies do you use?

I work with modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, and various CMS platforms. I choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and long-term goals.

Can you work with my existing website?

Definitely! I can redesign, update, or add new features to existing websites. Whether you need a refresh, new functionality, or performance improvements, I can work with your current setup or migrate to a better platform.

What's the process like working with you?

We start with a discovery call to understand your goals, then I provide a proposal and timeline. Once approved, we move through design, development, testing, and launch phases. I keep you updated throughout and welcome your feedback at every stage.
