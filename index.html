Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions we receive about our web development services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on complexity. A simple landing page takes 1-2 weeks, while a full e-commerce site with custom features can take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Are your websites mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Every website We build is fully responsive and fast for all devices - mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. We follow mobile-first design principles to ensure great user experience across all screen sizes.

Do you handle SEO Optimization?

Yes! We implement technical SEO best practices including proper meta tags, structured data, fast loading speeds, and mobile improvement. We can also help with content strategy and keyword research to improve your search rankings.

What technologies do you use?

We work with modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, and various CMS platforms. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and long-term goals.

Can you work with our existing website?

Definitely! We can redesign, update, or add new features to existing websites. Whether you need a refresh, new functionality, or performance optimizations, we can work with your current setup or migrate to a better platform.

What's the process like working with you?

We start with a discovery call to understand your goals, then we provide a proposal and timeline. Once approved, we move through design, development, testing, and launch phases. We keep you updated throughout and welcome your feedback at every stage.
