My Process

A proven 5-step process that ensures your project is delivered on time and exceeds expectations

1

Discovery & Planning

I often start with a detailed consultation to understand your business goals, target audience, and project requirements. I'll create a comprehensive project plan and timeline.

2

Design & Wireframing

I create detailed wireframes and mockups that visualize your website's structure and design. You'll see exactly how your site will look before development begins.

3

Development & Coding

Using the latest technologies and best practices, I build your website with clean, efficient code. Regular updates keep you informed of progress throughout development.

4

Testing & Optimization

Comprehensive testing across all devices and browsers ensures your website works perfectly. I optimize for speed, SEO, and user experience before launch.

5

Launch & Support

Your website goes live with full deployment support. I provide training on content management and offer ongoing maintenance to keep your site running smoothly.
