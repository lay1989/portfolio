SwiftBuild Infratech

SwiftBuild Infratech needed a comprehensive website to establish their presence in the international construction market. The project involved creating a professional portfolio that showcases their innovative construction techniques, completed projects, and expertise in modern infrastructure development.

Role

Full Stack Developer & SEO Specialist

Timeline

6 Weeks

Tools

HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, GSAP Animations

Problem

The client was losing international bids because their legacy site looked outdated. They needed a portfolio that matched the scale of their infrastructure projects while ranking for highly competitive international search terms.

Approach & Solution

We designed a timeless professional website focusing on brand positioning. Implemented GSAP animations for smooth interactions and engaging project showcases. Created comprehensive SEO optimization including meta tags, structured data, and content strategy. Built responsive layouts that work perfectly across all devices and implemented performance optimizations for fast loading.
